Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a car with Zoomcar?

Booking a car with Zoomcar is simple! You can book through our website or mobile app. Just select your location, choose the car you want, select your booking dates and times, and complete the payment process. Once booked, you'll receive a confirmation with all the details.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car with Zoomcar, you need a valid driver's license, a government-issued ID proof (Aadhaar, Passport, or PAN card), and a valid payment method. For international travelers, you'll need your passport and international driving permit along with your home country license.

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental price includes the car rental charges, comprehensive insurance, and standard maintenance. It does not include fuel costs, toll charges, parking fees, or traffic fines incurred during your rental period. Additional services like delivery and pickup may have extra charges.

Can I extend my booking during the trip?

Yes, you can extend your booking during your trip through the Zoomcar app or by calling our customer support. Please note that extensions are subject to vehicle availability and additional charges will apply based on the current rates.

What happens if I return the car late?

If you return the car later than your scheduled return time without prior extension, late return charges will apply. The charges are typically 1.5 times the hourly rate for the car. We recommend extending your booking through the app if you need more time to avoid these charges.
