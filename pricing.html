Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and offers

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental prices include the vehicle rental, comprehensive insurance, maintenance, and 24/7 roadside assistance. Fuel is not included in the base price - you pay only for the fuel you use.

How does the subscription plan work?

Subscription plans are monthly packages that give you a fixed number of driving hours each month. You can use these hours anytime during the month across multiple bookings. Additional hours beyond your plan are charged at discounted rates.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking up to 6 hours before your scheduled pickup time without any charges. Cancellations within 6 hours of pickup will incur a fee equivalent to 50% of the booking amount.

How do I apply promo codes?

You can apply promo codes during the booking process on our website or mobile app. Simply enter the promo code in the designated field before making payment. Each promo code has specific terms and validity periods.

Are there any hidden charges?

We believe in transparent pricing. All applicable charges including taxes are clearly displayed during the booking process. The only additional charges would be for fuel (if you use the car), tolls, and any traffic violations during your rental period.
