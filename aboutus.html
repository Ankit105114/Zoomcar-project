Our Story

From a simple idea to India's largest self-drive car rental platform

Revolutionizing Urban Mobility

Founded in 2013 by Greg Moran and David Back, Zoomcar was born out of a vision to transform urban mobility in India. Recognizing the challenges of car ownership in congested cities and the limitations of traditional car rentals, the founders set out to create a flexible, self-drive solution.

Zoomcar pioneered the self-drive car rental model in India, introducing the concept of renting cars by the hour, day, week, or month with complete freedom and flexibility. What started with just 7 cars in Bangalore has now grown to become India's leading self-drive mobility platform.

Today, Zoomcar operates in over 45 cities across India with a fleet of 10,000+ vehicles. Our innovative platform connects car owners with trusted drivers, creating new opportunities for both while reducing congestion and emissions in our cities.

Our Mission & Vision

At Zoomcar, our mission is to democratize access to vehicles and transform urban mobility across emerging markets. We envision a future where self-drive mobility solutions reduce congestion, lower emissions, and provide affordable transportation options for everyone.

We're building an ecosystem that empowers both car owners and drivers. Our platform enables individuals to monetize their idle cars while providing flexible, affordable mobility solutions to urban residents.

Through technology innovation and sustainable practices, we're working to create smarter, cleaner cities where transportation is accessible to all without the burdens of ownership.
